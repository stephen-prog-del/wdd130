Ghana

Ghana is a country in West Africa known for its rich history, diverse culture, and beautiful landscapes. It has a vibrant economy and is a major hub for trade in the region. Accra is the capital, and Ghana is famous for its cocoa production, gold resources, and warm hospitality.
