Our Mission

Rafting Travel (RT) is dedicated to providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences for all adventure seekers. With expert guides, top-quality equipment, and a passion for nature, we aim to create unforgettable memories while ensuring every guest feels secure and engaged.

History

Founded in 2010, Rafting Travel (RT) began with a vision to bring thrilling outdoor adventures to nature lovers. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted provider of unforgettable rafting experiences, combining our passion for the outdoors with the highest standards of safety and customer service.
